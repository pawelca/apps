Privacy Policy

NightCare is a privacy-first mobile application designed to help parents track newborn care in a calm and respectful way.

Data Collection

NightCare does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

  • No account creation
  • No email collection
  • No analytics
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No cloud sync
All data entered in NightCare stays locally on your device.

Local Storage

All information such as feeding logs, sleep events, and growth tracking is stored only on your device using local storage.

Deleting the app removes all stored data permanently.

Third-Party Services

NightCare does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or data collection services.

Children’s Privacy

NightCare is intended for parents and caregivers. The app does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, updates will be reflected on this page.
