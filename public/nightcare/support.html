Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. All data stays locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Do I need an account?

No. NightCare works without accounts, logins, or emails.

What happens if I change my phone?

Purchases can be restored via the App Store or Google Play, but local data is not synced unless you manually migrate your device.

Is NightCare a medical app?

No. NightCare is a logging and organization tool, not a medical or diagnostic application.
