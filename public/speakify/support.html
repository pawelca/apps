Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Speakify?

Simply tap the microphone button and start speaking. Your speech will be converted to text in real-time.

Is my data private?

Yes. All transcriptions stay on your device. We do not collect or store any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What languages are supported?

Speakify supports all languages available through your device's speech recognition system.

Can I export my transcriptions?

Yes. You can copy, share, or export your transcriptions using the built-in share functionality.
