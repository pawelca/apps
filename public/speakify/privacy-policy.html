Privacy Policy

Speakify is a privacy-focused mobile application designed to convert speech to text seamlessly and securely.

Data Collection

Speakify does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

  • No account creation
  • No email collection
  • No analytics
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No cloud sync
All transcriptions and data stay locally on your device.

Speech Recognition

Speakify uses on-device speech recognition whenever possible. If cloud-based recognition is used (depending on platform capabilities), audio is processed by the system provider (Apple/Google) according to their privacy policies.

Local Storage

All transcriptions, settings, and preferences are stored only on your device using local storage.

Deleting the app removes all stored data permanently.

Third-Party Services

Speakify does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or data collection services.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, updates will be reflected on this page.
